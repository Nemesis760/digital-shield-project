About The Project

Mission & Goals

Digital Detective Academy addresses the growing need for cybersecurity awareness. Unlike traditional methods, we use a gamified approach where students become detectives.

  • Target Audience: Middle School (11-13 Years)
  • Method: Story-Driven Scenarios
  • Outcome: Digital Literacy
